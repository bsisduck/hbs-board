# HBS Board — Multi-Persona Workflows

## Board Meeting: Full Business Review

Run personas sequentially for a comprehensive business assessment:

1. **Porter** → Competitive position and industry analysis
2. **Kellogg** → Marketing strategy and customer acquisition
3. **Wharton** → Financial health check and ratio analysis
4. **MIT Sloan** → Operations audit and efficiency gains
5. **Stanford GSB** → Leadership and team assessment
6. **INSEAD** → Growth roadmap and expansion plan

## Decision Pipeline

For any major strategic decision:

1. **HBS Case Study** → Find a historical parallel and extract lessons
2. **Kennedy School** → Structure the decision with matrix and bias audit
3. **Harvard Negotiation** → Prepare for the deal execution
4. **Columbia/Chicago Booth** → Model the financial implications

## Startup Launch Sequence

For new ventures or major pivots:

1. **Christensen** → Validate the disruption opportunity
2. **Porter** → Assess competitive landscape
3. **Kellogg** → Build go-to-market strategy
4. **Columbia** → Design the funding strategy
5. **MIT Sloan** → Design scalable operations

## Crisis Response

For urgent business problems:

1. **Kennedy School** → Frame the decision under pressure
2. **Porter** → Assess competitive implications
3. **Wharton** → Analyze financial impact
4. **Harvard Negotiation** → Negotiate with stakeholders

## Scaling Playbook

For businesses ready to scale:

1. **MIT Sloan** → Identify operational bottlenecks
2. **INSEAD** → Map expansion opportunities
3. **Stanford GSB** → Build the leadership team
4. **Chicago Booth** → Structure the financing
5. **Kellogg** → Scale customer acquisition
